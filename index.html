    Academic Background

    I am a junior at Cornell University pursuing a double major in Economics and Information Science with a data science concentration. I enjoy actively working on projects and communicating insights to diverse audiences. Through courses, internships, pre-professional leadership experiences, I have built strong analytical and communication skills.

    My coursework spans business, economics, mathematics, data science, and communication. Despite the technical nature of my courseload, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with peers on many technical group projects that have helped me develop stronger teamwork skills and learn to communicate technical concepts to people of different levels.

    Two classes that have been especially impactful are INFO 2950 (Introduction to Data Science) and ECON 3120 (Applied Econometrics). Both courses showed me how there is so much more to data and its applications in the world than I had ever known before. I got the opportunity to collect data, create research questions, build models and regressions, conduct hypothesis testing, and present my insights in a structured way.
