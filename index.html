    About Me

    Growing up, I was homeschooled across the United States and Hong Kong before returning to the US for high school. That experience shaped how I approach learning and work to this day. Being homeschooled gave me the opportunity to begin studying classical piano at the age of 4; studying piano taught me to be detail-oriented and disciplined, carefully working through each note and musical phrase until I had fully mastered a piece, and building the consistency to practice for two hours every day regardless of the season or workload. Because so much of my education was self-directed, I developed a strong intrinsic motivation to learn. That drive has carried into my academic and professional life, and it is a big part of why I am comfortable setting my own goals, managing my time, and pursuing subjects in depth simply out of curiosity.
